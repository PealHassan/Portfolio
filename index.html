Publications

Simplifying Student Queries: A Dialogflow-Based Conversational Chatbot for University Websites

Developed and published a research paper on an AI-powered conversational chatbot aimed at enhancing communication within university communities. The project addresses inefficiencies in traditional communication by providing a 24/7 support system for students, faculty, and staff. Built using Google Dialogflow, the chatbot leverages intents, entities, and contexts to understand and respond to queries effectively. Key objectives include improving administrative efficiency, enhancing user experience, and promoting greater engagement with university services. The system is designed to reduce workload on support staff and create a more connected and responsive academic environment.
